Homemade Mozzarella Sticks

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sea Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Eggs
  • Panko
  • Italian seasoning
  • Mozzarella cheese or string cheese

Directions:

  1. Get three bowls.
  2. Put flour, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in the first bowl. Put whisked egg with a little water in the second. Put Panko and Italian seasoning in the third.
  3. Dip each piece of cheese in the bowls in this order: egg, flour, egg, panko.
  4. Freeze for 45 minutes.
  5. Once the oil temperature is 350Â°F, you can fry the mozzarella sticks.
  6. Let them cool.
  7. Serve with marinara sauce and enjoy!
