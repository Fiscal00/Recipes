One-pan Creamy Chicken and Spinach Dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 1tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 minced clove garlic
  • 1/3 cup chicken broth
  • 14oz diced tomatoes
  • 5oz cubed cheese
  • 1tsp Italian seasoning
  • 4 cups fresh spinach
  • 2tbsp minced chives

Directions:

  1. Slice chicken breasts into cutlets, season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Add oil and butter to a pan. Once butter is melted, mix them together.
  3. Lightly brown chicken, turn once, 2-3 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate.
  4. Same skillet: add onion and cook 2 minutes, add garlic and wine. Stir until evaporated.
  5. Stir in tomatoes, juice, cheese, and Italian seasoning. Stir 5 minutes, add spinach.
  6. Add chicken back to the skillet and stir.
  7. Garnish with chives.
