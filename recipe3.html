Chicken Quesadilla Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Whole wheat tortilla
  • Chili powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Mozzarella cheese or string cheese

Directions:

  1. Add chicken to a bowl and add half of the spices.
  2. Saute the chicken with remaining spices.
  3. Add fillings on one half of the tortilla and fold.
  4. Cook quesadilla in a skillet until golden brown.
  5. Enjoy!
